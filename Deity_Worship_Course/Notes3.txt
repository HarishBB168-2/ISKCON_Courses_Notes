- Vastvik yogyata vigraha sewa ki
* Srila Prabhupada says - brahman can only
do vigraha sewa.
* Mukt from - kaam, krodh, lobh, moha, mad,
matsarya
* Drid iccha is also required.

- If not yogya then
* Take permission and guidance from bhakt
* Then Bhagwan accepts the sewa
* Drid iccha is also required.

- Minimum requirements
* Bhagwan ko subah uthana
* Doing mangalaarti
* Agar vigraha hai to, snan karaye, 
kapade pehnaye.
* Fir bhog lagaye
* Jitne baar khud khate hai, utni baar
bhagwan ko arpit kare.
* Agar ghar par nahi hai, aur bahar hai,
to bhagwan ke liye cooking hona hoga.
> Vigraha ek vyakti hai.

apne gale mai tang ke rakhte hai.
> Bhakt bhagwan ke bacche ke samaan sewa

- Uthapan - Bhagwan ko uthana
* Baccho ko kaise uthaya jata hai

> Kuch mantro ka uccharan kiya

> Mantro ki list hai
>> Panchratra Vigraha Archan Pradeep 
(Deity Worship Manual)-Hindi
> Alag vigraho ka alag alag mantra hai
* Isi tarah sulane ke liye mantra hai
* Isi tarah khilane ke liye mantra hai
* Sabse pehle achman bhi kiya jata hai
> Ideal standard is to do snan, then tilak,
then 12 achman karke, then enter into mandir
for sewa.
> Minimum 3 achman are required.
> There is list of achman.
* Pehle jal liya jata hai, aur jal ko shuddh
kiya jata hai mantra se
गङ्गे च यमुने चैव गोदावरि सरस्वति।
नर्मदे सिन्धु कावेरि जलेऽस्मिन् संनिधिं कुरु॥

O Ganges, Yamuna, Godāvari, Sarasvatī, 
Narmadā, Sindhu and Kāverī, please 
make your presence felt in this water.
> Fir jal ko hath mai lekar paan kiya jata
hai mantra uccharan karte hue
> 3 achman
>> Om Keshavaya Namah
>> Om Narayanaya Namah
>> Om Madhavaya Namah
> Hath dhone ke liye
>> Om Govindaya Namah
>> Om Vishnave Namah

> Srila Prabhupada Pranam Mantra
> Shri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu Pranam Mantra
namo mahā-vadānyāya
kṛṣṇa-prema-pradāya te
kṛṣṇāya kṛṣṇa-caitanya-
nāmne gaura-tviṣe namaḥ

“O most munificent incarnation! 
You are Kṛṣṇa Himself appearing as 
Śrī Kṛṣṇa Caitanya Mahāprabhu. You have
assumed the golden color of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī, 
and You are widely distributing pure 
love of Kṛṣṇa. We offer our respectful 
obeisances unto You.
> Shri Krishna Pranam Mantra
namo brahmaṇya-devāya
go-brāhmaṇa-hitāya ca
jagad-dhitāya kṛṣṇāya
govindāya namo namaḥ

“ ‘Let me offer my respectful obeisances 
unto Lord Kṛṣṇa, who is the worshipable 
Deity for all brahminical men, who is the
well-wisher of cows and brāhmaṇas, and 
who is always benefiting the whole world. 
I offer my repeated obeisances to the
Personality of Godhead, known as Kṛṣṇa 
and Govinda.’

>> These are subtle methods to purify bhoga
> Leave bhoga for some time
>> Chhota bhoga - 5-7 minutes
>>> Milk, fruit
>> Full prasad - 15-20 minutes
>>> Roti, sabji, daal, chaval

> After waiting time take away the prasadam

achman karana, fir bhagwan ko apne asan par
virajman karenge.
