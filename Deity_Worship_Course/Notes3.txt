Day 3
Speaker : HG Avatar Gaur Prabhuji
Topic - How to do puja of Vigraha

- Vigraha sewa
* Uthapan
* Bhog lagana
* Snan
* Archan
* Aarti
* Shayan

- Vastvik yogyata vigraha sewa ki
* Srila Prabhupada says - brahman can only
do vigraha sewa.
* Mukt from - kaam, krodh, lobh, moha, mad,
matsarya
* Drid iccha is also required.

- If not yogya then
* Take permission and guidance from bhakt
* Then Bhagwan accepts the sewa
* Drid iccha is also required.

- Bhagwan ki sthapna
* Bhagwan ke naam ka kirtan karke
* Bhakto ko bulake aur unse bhagwan
ka abhishek kara kar
> Agar painting/chitra hai to sirf
flower se abhishek se karte hai

- After sthapna
* Vastra, alankar, snan patra, 
* Aarti ke liye avashyak samagri
> Shankh, ghanti, jal arpit karne ke 
liye shankh, Aarti-panch, Panch-batti wali aarti,
agarbatti, flower
> Bhog lagane ke liye vyavastha.

- Uthapan (Bhagwan ko uthana)
* Before mangalaarti
* Bhagwan ko uthaya jata hai
* Fir bhog lagaya jata hai
* Fir mangalaarti kiya jata hai
* Fir vigraha ka snan/archan kiya jata hai
> Shodash-upchar vidhi
> Dwadash-upchar
> Pancho-upchar

- Darshan aarti
- Guru-puja
- Bhagwan ko bhog
- Bal-bhog aarti
- Raaj-bhog aarti
* Pehle bhog lagega
* Then aarti hogi
- Dopahar mai shayan
* 12:30 to 01:00 pm
- Uthapan
* 04:00 to 04:30
* Pehle bhagwan ko bhog lagta hai
* Then uthaya jata hai
* Then dhoop aarti kiya jata hai
- Sandhya bhog
- Sandhya/Gaur aarti
- Doodh ka bhog
- Chota aarti
- Shayan aarti
- Bhagwan ko sula diya jata hai
* Kapat band ho jate hai
* Bhagwan ko dressing kiya jata hai
* Then sula diya jata hai

- Mandir mai 7 baar bhog lagta hai
* Bhog lagne ke baad aarti hoti hai
* Bhog banane ke liye alag vyavastha hoti hai
* Vigraha ka kitchen alag hota hai

- Mandir ki vidhi ko pura-pura palan 
ghar mai nahi kar sakte
* Ghar mai kuch relaxation diya gaya hai
* Apni chamta ke anusar sewa kar sakta hai

- Minimum requirements
* Bhagwan ko subah uthana
* Doing mangalaarti
* Agar vigraha hai to, snan karaye, 
kapade pehnaye.
* Fir bhog lagaye
* Jitne baar khud khate hai, utni baar
bhagwan ko arpit kare.
* Agar ghar par nahi hai, aur bahar hai,
to bhagwan ke liye cooking hona hoga.

Completed till 20:00
