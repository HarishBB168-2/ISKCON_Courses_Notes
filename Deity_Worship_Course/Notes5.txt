Day 5
Speaker : HG Avatar Gaur Prabhuji
Topic - Sewa mai hone vale apradh - 2

- 32 Apradho ko atirikt suchi Varah-puran mai hai
1. Andhkar mai Vigraha ko sparsh na kare.
2. Vigraha-archan ke vidhi-vidhan ke dridh paalan
mai pramad na kare.
> Vidhi-vidhan mai apne man se change na kare.
> Hume bhakto and acharyo se puchana chahiye.
3. Dhwani kiye bina mandir mai pravisht na ho.
4. Kutte aadi adham pashuo ki drishti se dushit
padarth bhagwan ko arpit na kare.
> Bhog ko dhak kar le jaye, aur arpit kare.
5. Aaradhana ke kaal mai maunbhang na kare.
> Apas mai baat na kare.
> Katha aur kirtan sun sakte hai.
6. Pujan karte hue mutra-puri-shotsarg na kare.
> Meaning - in between of puja toilet na jaye.
7. Pushp ka arpan kiye bina dhup na dikhana
> Jab aarti karte hai to sabse pehle pushp arpit
karte hai.
> Agar pushp na ho to, dhup ya agarbatti arpit
nahi kar sakte, keval deepak arpit kar sakte hai.
> Sabse pehle bhagwan ke charno mai chota sa ek 
pushp arpit kiya jata hai, then fir dhup dikhaya
jata hai.
8. Sugandh-rahit pushpo ko arpit na kare.
> Also artificial flowers ko bhi arpit na kare
9. Pratidin acchi prakar se dant-dhavan (dantmanjan)
karna
> Daat saaf nahi karna bhi apradh hai.
10. Maithun (sambhog) ke baad tatkal mandir mai 
pravesh na karna.
> Maithun (sambhog) kriya ke baad snan karna chahiye, 
and clean clothes pahanana chahiye.
11. Rajasvala (masik-dharm/periods) stri ka sparsh
na karna.
> Mandir aa sakte hai, par Vigraha sewa karne wale
pujario ka sparsh nahi kar sakte.
12. Mrit-deha ka sparsh karke mandir mai pravesh na
karna.
> Chote chote kide ke liye applicable nahi hai, 
example - machar etc.
> But animals like - birds, big animal etc. this
niyam is applicable.
> Sparsh karne ke baad snan karna.
13. Laal (red), nile (blue), asvach (unclean) vastro
ko dharan kiye hue mandir mai pravesh na karna.
> In vastro ko pehne hue bhagwan ki sewa nahi kar 
sakte.
14. Mrit-deha ka darshan karke mandir mai na jana.
> Uske baad snan karna chahiye.
15. Mandir mai adho-vayu na chhodna.
16. Mandir mai krodh na karna.
17. Shamshan jakar mandir mai na jana.
> Shamshan yatra mai agar sammilit hue he, to
mandir mai snan karke jana chahiye.
18. Vigraha ke aage dakaar na lena.
> Jab tak bhojan ka purna paachan na ho gaya ho,
mandir mai pravesh na kare.
> Evening mai jaldi prasad le lena chahiye
> Jab dakaar aaye to Bhagwan se kshama prarthna 
karni chahiye.
> Jab dakaar/ubaasi aye to muha pe haath laga lena
chahiye. Fir haath ko dho lena chahiye.
> Sharir ke kai jagah se mal nikalta hai - ear, eye,
nose, mouth, kamar se niche, paero ko hath lagate hai.
To haath ashudh ho jata hai, to hath ko dho lena 
chahiye.
19. Charas, gaanja ka prayog na kare.
20. Afim aadi madya padartho ka sewan na kare.
21. Sharir par tel (oil) lagakar mandir mai pravesh 
ya vigraha ka sparsh na kare.
> Sharir par tel maalish karke snan karna hota hai.
> But soap se snan karenge to oil hat jaat hai.
> So, bhakt bathroom mai hi oil rakhte hai. Snan karne
ke baad oil lagaa lete hai, then again body par paani
daal lete hai, jisase shuddh ho jaye.
22. Bhagwan param ishwar hai - aisa pratipadan karne 
wale kisi bhi shashtra ka apmaan na kare.
23. Kisi virodhi shashtra ka pravartan na kare. 
> Bhagwan param ishwar hai - iske alawa koi aur 
nishkarsh nikalne wale shashtro ko mahatva nahi dena
chahiye.
24. Vigraha ke aage paan na chabaye.
25. Ashuddh patra mai rakhe pushp ko na chadaye.
> Saaf-suthri tokri etc use karni chahiye.
> Arpit (offered) kiye hue pushp aur naye pushp (unoffered)
mix nahi hone chahiye.
26. Aasan ke bina bhumi par baith kar Bhagavad-Archan
na kare.
> Kapade ka, kuss grass ka etc
> Aasan shuddhi bhi kare.
27. Khud snan pura karne se purva vigraha ka sparsh na kare.
> Also snan (vigraha ka snan) pura karne se purva vigraha
ka sparsh na kare.
> Vigraha par pani dalte vakt, bich-bich mai vigraha ko
touch na kare.
> Bhagwan ke snan mai left hand ka upyog nahi karte hai.
> Bhagwan ke dressing mai left hand ka kam se kam upyog kare.
28. Mastak (forehead) par tin rekha ka tilak (trikund)
na lagae.
> Viashnav urdhva-pundrak tilak dharan karte hai.
29. Haath-paer dhoye bina mandir mai pravesh na kare.
> Kuch khane ke baad muha mai kulla karna chahiye.
30. Avaishnav dvara bhojan ka arpan na kare.
> Market se lakar bhagwan ko bhog na lagae.
> Market wali chijo ko kam-se-kum arpit kare.
31. Abhakt ke samne Vigraha-archan na kare.
> Isiliye pat band karke archan hota hai.
>> Archan -> bhog lagana, snan karana
> Prarambh mai Ganapati ki upasna kare
>> Jisase marg mai anewale sab vighn door ho jate hai.
>> Unko Bhagwan mankar upasna na kare.
>> Ganpati Ji, Bhagwan Nrisimha ke charankamlo ke 
upasak hai.
32. Nakho (nakhun/fingernail) and ungalio (finger) ke
sparsh se dushit jal mai Vigraha ko snan nahi karana 
chahiye.
> Jis jal se Bhagwan ko snan karate hai, use nakhun/finger
se touch nahi karna chahiye.
> Agar temperature check karna hai to knuckles (por) ki side
se check kare.

Completed till 32:00
