> Radha Krishna
agaccha sayana-sthanam priyabhih saha kesava 
divya-puspatya-sayyayam sukham vihara madhava

Now come, O Kesava, along with Your
beloved Srimati Radharani and Her friends, to 
the bed covered with transcendental, aromatic flowers.
Now happily enjoy Your pastimes, O Madhava.
* Bhagwan ke bade vigraha ko shayan 
khade-khade hi diya jata hai.
* Bhagwan ke utsav vigraha chote hote
hai, unke liye bistar hota hai, uske
upar unko sula diya jata hai.
* Shringar aarti ke dress hata kar, 
simple dress pehnaya jata hai, jo
mangalaarti mai dress dikhti hai.
* Raat ki dressing mai bhagwan ko purna
snan nahi karaya jata.
> Pehle gile kapde, then sukhe kapde se
bhagwan ko pochkar, saaf karke, dusre
vastra pehna diye jate hai.
* Fir bhagwan ko hath, paer, muh dhula kar,
achman kara kar, fir kuch bhog lagate hai - 
rabdi ka.
> Kahi bhog sath me rakh diya jata hai 
puri raat ke liye.
> Kahi par bhog lagane ke baad hata diya
jata hai.
* Bhog lagane ke baad sula diya jata hai.
Sulane ke liye bhi mantra hote hai.
