- 32 Niyam
1. Bhagwan ke mandir mai vahan par baithe hue
athva jute pehne-pehne pravesh nahi karna
chahiye.
2. Bhagwan ki prasannata ke liye janmastmi,
rathyatra, aadi mahotsavo ko avashya manana.
> Ghar mai jo vigraha rakhe hai, unke mukhya
utsav avashya manana chahiye.
> Jaise Gaur-Nitai hai, to Gaur Purnima utsav,
Nityananda Triyodashi utsav manana chahiye.
>> Unka abhishek karna aadi.
> Apni chamta ke anusar. Kuch-na-kuch karyakram
karna chahiye
> Utsav nahi manana ek apradh mai ataa hai.
3. Vigraha ke aage dandvat pranam karne mai
pramad na karna.
4. Bhojan ke baad hath-paer dhoye bina mandir
mai pravesh na karna.
> Bhojan ke baad sharir ashudh ho jaata hai.
> Mandir ke standard bai bhojan ke baad snan
karne ke baad vigraha kaksh mai pravesh karte hai.
> Baki sabke liye kam se kam, muh dhona, 
kulla karna, hath, paer dhona.
5. Dushit avastha mai mandir-pravesh na karna.
> Vaidik shashtra ke anusar
> Parivar mai kisi ki mrityu ho jane par
sampurna kul kuch kaal ke liye dushit ho jata hai.
> Brahman-kul ke liye dushitkaal 12 din rahta hai.
> Kshatriya and Vaishyo ke liye 15 din
> Shudro ke liye 30 din
> Iss dushit kaal mai, vigraha sewa ke liye
kisi anya vyakti ko jimmedari deni chahiye,
ki vo ghar akar ke sewa kare, ya vigraha ko 
unko de dena chahiye sewa karne ke liye.
> Isiliye sabhi logo se, aaspas ke logo se,
bhakto se accha sambandh bana kar rakhna chahiye.
> Shamshan mai jaane se, Mrit sharir ka sparsh karne se
ashuddh ho jate hai. Suddhi ke liye snan karna chahiye.
Kapdo ko bhi dhone chahiye.
> Mal ka sparsh karne se ashuddhi aa jati hai, keval
gaye ke gobar ko chor kar. Suddhi ke liye snan 
karna chahiye.
6. Ek hath se dandwat pranam na karna.
7. Shri Krishna ke aage parikrama na karna
> Mandir ki parikrama ki vidhi yaha hai ki Shri-Vigraha
ki dakshin disha se pradakshina kare. Mandir ke bahar
pratidin 3 baar parikrama karne chahiye.
> Vigraha ke aage gol-gol ghumna (rotating oneself instead of
revolving around Vigraha) (chakkar khana) nahi chahiye
> Vigraha ke samne hum khade ho, to Vigraha ke right side se
(humare left side se) parikrama shuru karni hai.
8. Vigraha ke aage paer na failae, (specially talve bhagwan
ke taraf nahi hone chahiye).
9. Vigraha ke sammukh ukadva (murge vali position) nahi
baithna chahiye.
10. Shri Krishna Vigraha ke aage lete nahi.
> Jab mandir chota ho space kam he, aur temple hall mai
hi sona hota hai, to jab pat band ho jaye tab sona hota hai.
11. Vigraha ke aage prasad na khae
> Mandir mai to nahi khana chahiye
> Agar Ghar mai he to prasad mandir ke pat band karke 
khilana chahiye.
12. Vigraha ke aage jhuth nahi bolna chahiye.
13. Vigraha ke samaksh jor se na bole.
14. Vigraha ke aage baat na kare
> Bhagwan ke katha and sewa ke alawa aur
kuch baat nahi karna chahiye
15. Vigraha ke aage rudan athva chitkar na kare.
16. Vigraha ke sammukh ladai-jhagda na kare.
17. Vigraha ke samaksh kisi ko date nahi
18. Vigraha ke aage bhiksu ko daan na de.
> Prasad dena allowed hai.
19. Vigraha ke aage kisi se kathor vachan na kahe.
20. Vigraha ke aage charm (leather) dharan na kare.
21. Vigraha ke sannidhya mai kisi anya ki stuti
na kare.
> Isiliye kisi anya bhakt ko pranam karna bhi
Vigraha ke samaksh varjit hai.
22. Vigraha ke nikat koi apshabd na kahe.
23. Vigraha ke nikat adho-vayu (paet mai gas) na chhode.
24. Vaibhav ke anusar Vigraha ki archana mai 
pramad na kare.
> BG mai bhagwan kehte hai ki ve bhakt dwara samarpit
jal ya patte se bhi santhusht ho jate hai.
> Bhagwan ne yaha sarvbhaum vidhan kiya hai.
> Param akinchan manushya bhi iska palan kar sakta hai.
> Parantu iska arth yaha nahi hai ki vaibhavpurna
archan karne mai samarth purush bhi Bhagwan ko keval
jal aur patra-pushp se tusht kare.
> Vaibhav ke anusar sundar abhushano, pusho, 
nai-vedyo (bhog) ka arpan karna chahiye.
> Aisa nahi ki Bhagwan ko to jal aur patra-pushp se
prasann kare aur sare dhan ka apne indriya-tripti
mai vyay kare.
25. Shri Krishna ko arpan kiye bina kuch na khaye.
26. Ritu (season) ke fal aur ann ka Shri Krishna ko
nivedan karna na bhule.
> Agar chamta hai, season mai aane vale fal aur ann
jaise - khajur etc to use Bhagwan ko bhog lagana 
chahiye.
> Agar aisa na kare to Bhagwan isase khus nahi hote.
Meri prakriti itna de rahi hai, use bhi bhog lagana
chahiye.
> Jaha tak chamta hai utna jarur karna chahiye.
27. Vigraha ko bhog lagane se purv kisi ko bhi na
khilaye.
28. Vigraha ke aur peeth na kare.
> Bhagwan ke sammukh hona chahiye.
29. Guru ko pranam chup-chap na kare, arthat pranam
karte hue Guru-Pranati ka ucch swar se path kare.
> Jisko pranam kar rahe hai, unko pata chalna chahiye.
> Vaani ka use karna chahiye.
30. Guru ke saanidhya mai stav-paath karna na bhule.
> Bhagwan ke bhakto and Guru ke samne unki stuti
karna chahiye.
> Unke samne apna Gun-gaan nahi karna chahiye.
31. Guru ke aage aatm-prashansha na kare.
32. Vigraha ke samaksh dev-ninda (Devtao) na kare
> Devta Bhagwan ke bhakt hai.
