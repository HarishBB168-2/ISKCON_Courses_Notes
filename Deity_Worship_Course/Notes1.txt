Day 1
Speaker : HG Avatar Gaur Prabhuji
Topic - Importance of Deity Worship for Grihastha Ashrama
(Deity Worship ki mahima)

- 
This subject to be understood and listened again and again by
everyone.

- Why is Deity Worship Important?
* Deity is avatar of God.
* God out of mercy come as Deity.
* Common man can see the God as Deity.
* Vigraha roop Bhagwan se abhinn hai.
* Bhagwan ka naam, roop, guna aur lila
bhagwan se abhinn hai.
* Bhakti ke 64 ang and navdha-bhakti mai
vigraha sewa aati hai.
* Shri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu said that
bhakti ke 64 me se 5 ang important hai.
Inme se koi ek hi paryapt hai.
> Bhakt sang
> Bhagwat shravan/pathan
> Chanting
> Vigraha sewa
> Mathura vaas
* Navdha-bhakti ka hi expansion he 
Bhakti ke 64 ang. Yehe hume Navdha-bhakti
ko acche se samjhane ke liye hai.

- Narad panchratra

- Vigraha sewa should be done in home under
guidance of a bhakt. And not in manmaana way.

- Ghar ko mandir banane ke liye, bhagwan
ki avashyakta he, isiliye vigraha sthapit
karna chahiye.

- Ashram meaning
* Jaha par bhagwan ki prapti ke liye
samyak roop se shram/mehnat kiya jaat hai.
* So grihasth ashram ka bhi isilye use
karna chahiye.

- Grihasth ashram ke khatre
* Directly in material dealings
* Mai/Meri ki bhawna bahut aasani
se aa-jati hai.
* Various danger mentioned in 
SB 5th and 7th canto.
* So, vigraha sewa is very important.

- Vigraha aur Bhagwan mai koi antar 
nahi hai
* Ghar me vigraha ko ham thakur ji
kehte hai
* Ghar ke malik ke roop me unko
prati-sthapit kiya jata hai.
* Jisse hamare andar malik ka bhaav
vo change ho jaye.
* Vigraha ko ghar ke main jagah 
par rakhte hai
> Khulli jagah ho
> jaha par bhagwan ke baar-baar
darshan ho sake.
> Jaha par kai log ikkhate ho sake
or baith sake, and kirtan kar sake.
* Bhagwan ke upar ashrit hone ka
bhaav aayega.
> Bhagwan hi chala rahe hai.
> Bhagwan hi ghar ke malik hai.
* Vigraha roop mai bhagwan sewa
lene aate hai.
* Vigraha sewa, hamare andar jo
bhog ka vichar hai use sewa ke
vichar mai parivartit karta hai.

- Ghar mai rahne vali sabhi logo
ko sewa ka mauka milta hai.
* Ghar mai agar koi ek vyakti bhakti
kar raha hai.
* Vigraha important bhumika nibhate 
hai ghar ke sabhi logo ko sewa mai
lagane ke liye.
* Prasad sabhi ko baatne se sabhi
ka shuddi-karan hota hai.
* Bhagwan ko phool chadate hai, uss
phool ko sabhi ko sunghne ke liye 
dete hai, usase sabhi ka suddhi
karan hota hai.
* Bhagwan ka kirtan karte hai,
sabhi log kirtan ko sunte hai,
sabhi ka shuddi-karan hota hai.

- Tension etc. problem ko dur karne
ke liye ghar mai vigraha hona chahiye
* Amangal dur rahega
* But agar bhagwan ghar mai hai, aur
sewa nahi ho rahi hai, to tension 
dur nahi hoga. Bhagwan se connect
nahi hoga.
* Sewa karoge tabhi bhagwan se connect
kar payenge.

- BG 12.8
मय्येव मन आधत्स्व मयि बुद्धिं निवेशय ।
निवसिष्यसि मय्येव अत ऊर्ध्वं न संशय: ॥ ८ ॥

Just fix your mind upon Me,
the Supreme Personality of Godhead, 
and engage all your intelligence in Me. 
Thus you will live in Me always,
without a doubt.

- Vigraha sewa karke hi hum samajh
sakte hai ki vigraha hi bhagwan hai
