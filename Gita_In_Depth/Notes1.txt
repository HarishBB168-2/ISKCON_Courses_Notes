- Shloka 1.1

धृतराष्ट्र उवाच
धर्मक्षेत्रे कुरुक्षेत्रे समवेता युयुत्सव: ।
मामका: पाण्डवाश्चैव किमकुर्वत सञ्जय ॥ १ ॥

* Dhritrashtra - One who have hold tight the rashtra,
my - family, possessions, ghar-bhar, children, raaj-path,
samridhi, safalta
> Symbolize - Samananiya vyakti, who has hold tight to his 
world, and thus is in tanaav and dukh. 
Symbolize general person.
> Unable to go for sadhu sang and divya gyan.  
* Why dharmkshetre
> Long time ago there was a king named Kuru.
> Kuru wanted to farm Dharma here.
> Bhagawan came to take his pariksha.
> Kuru have his everything to Bhagawan.
> Then Bhagawan said - as you want increase dharma here,
so any work done here will be according to dharma, and
ultimately dharma will be victorious.
> So named as Kurukshetre and Dharmkshetre
* Where there is Krishna, there is Dharma also.
> So victory will also be there.
* 2 big reasons in favour of Pandavas
> Fight is in Dharmkshetra
> Krishna is also with Pandavas
* A leader should not be partial with anyone.
* Dhritrashtra is an example of weak parenting
* Questions
> Why first shlok by Dhritrashtra?
>> Dhritrashtra is showing interest in listening
>> Symoblizes a common person.
>> So we should show such interest of listening
>> First word is "Dharma". Because Dharma is main point. 
Knowing our real Dharma

- Shloka 1.2

सञ्जय उवाच
दृष्ट्वा तु पाण्डवानीकं व्यूढं दुर्योधनस्तदा ।
आचार्यमुपसङ्गम्य राजा वचनमब्रवीत् ॥ २ ॥

* Sanjay 
> Is disciple of Shri Vyas Dev
> Got Divya drishti with special mercy of Shri Vyas Dev
> He got Varadan
>> He will not get any injury and not die during fight in Mahabharata.
>> He will also able to listen and see all events in Yuddh-Kshetra
>> And in evening and sometimes in betweeen come and tell
Dhritrashtra all events of Yuddh.
* Shri Vedvyas Ji first offered Divya Dhristi to Dhritrashtra
> Dhritrashtra refused to take it and asked to give to Sanjay
> As he don't wanted to see killing of his children in yuddh
* Shri Yudhisthir Maharaj also went to Shri Dronacharya and
Shri Bhism-pitamaha.
> He removed shield, bow-arrow
t> With ordinary attire
> By walking
> Asked for ashirvaad, with hath-jodkarke, vineet bhav me
> Shri Dronacharya also said, if you haven't came for ashirvad, then
I would have cursed for your defeat. But as you came for
ashirvad, I give you ashirvad of victory.
* Duryodhan went toward Shri Dronacharya as army general or king.
> Seeing them as warriors, which are to be managed and inspired.

- Shloka 1.3

पश्यैतां पाण्डुपुत्राणामाचार्य महतीं चमूम् ।
व्यूढां द्रुपदपुत्रेण तव शिष्येण धीमता ॥ ३ ॥

* Duryodhan is taunting 
* Dristidryumn son of Drupad has arranged the army of Pandavas 
* Story
> Drupad and Shri Dronacharya used to study in same gurukul and became friends
> Drupad once said that you are my friend and whenever you have any
requirement of anything just ask me for help.
> After gurukul time, Shri Dronacharya was in poor condition, he was unable to 
give much to his son Ashwatthama.
> One day some children has given chuna ka pani as milk to Ashwatthama. He 
vomitted all of it.
> After seeing this Shri Dronacharya remembered Drupad's promise. So, he went to
his Friend.
> But Drupad, refused his friendship with Shri Dronacharya. 
> After seeing Drupad's ghamand, he decided to humble down Drupad.
> After training Kaurav and Pandav, he asked gurudakshina of arresting Drupad 
and bringing in front of him.
> First Kaurav went with his army for the task, but were unsuccessfull.
> Then Pandav went without any army for the task, and defeated Drupad and
arrested Drupad. And bring him in front of Shri Dronacharya.
> Shri Dronacharya has freed Drupad from arrest.
> Drupad became angry and decided to kill Shri Dronacharya.
> Drupad hosted a yagya to get a son (named Dristidriyumn) who will 
kill Shri Dronacharya.
> Dristidriyumn went to Shri Dronacharya to learn combat skills. 
Shri Dronacharya already knew that Drupad has got this son to kill me.
Still Shri Dronacharya accepted Dristidriyumn as his disciple.
* Duryodhan is saying that due to your kindness you taught combat skills
to that person. And he has created a great battle formation.

- Shloka 1.6

युधामन्युश्च विक्रान्त उत्तमौजाश्च वीर्यवान् ।
