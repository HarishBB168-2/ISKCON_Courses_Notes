janane ki vidhi

- Mana rupi darpana ke upar gandagi/dhul
* Gandagi/dhul - Bhog ki icchaye
* Bhog ki icchaye ayi he baar-baar bhog
karne se.

- Mana pavitra ho raha he ka matlab he
* Bhagwan ke iccha ke ansuar karm karne
ki pravritti hridaya me badh rahi he.

- Mana pavitra he to
* Hum bhagwan ko hridaya me dekh payenge.
* Praman - BG 6.7
जितात्मन: प्रशान्तस्य परमात्मा समाहित: ।
शीतोष्णसुखदु:खेषु तथा मानापमानयो: ॥ ७ ॥

For one who has conquered the mind, 
the Supersoul is already reached, 
for he has attained tranquillity. 
To such a man happiness and distress, 
heat and cold, honor and dishonor 
are all the same.

- Sacche sukh me sthapit ho sankenge

Mai yaha sharir nahi hu, mai atma hu
aur chaitnya hi mera swarup-laxan he.

- Bhautik dukho ki jwala ka bhujana
* Tabhi hoga jab tak hum apne adhyatmik
jivan mai-sudh chetna mai sthit nahi
ho jate.

- CC Madhya 20.117
kṛṣṇa bhuli’ sei jīva anādi-bahirmukha
ataeva māyā tāre deya saṁsāra-duḥkha

“Forgetting Kṛṣṇa, the living entity has 
been attracted by the external feature from 
time immemorial. Therefore the illusory 
energy [māyā] gives him all kinds of misery
in his material existence.

- Meaning of Dharma
* Kisi chiz ka vehe guna jo usse alag nahi
kiya ja sakta.
* Jaise jal ki taralta

- Never think you are controller of shelter taker
* Cultivate attitude of service
* If attitude that we are servant is rooted in consciousness
then false ego will never rise.

- Hamari Sewavritti ka vibhinn upadhiyon me durupyog
kiya ja ra ha he.
* Upadhi - husband, wife, prime minister, etc
* Whole life sewa-vritti is exhausted in those upadhis,
instead of using it for God.
* Kya insab post ko chodh ke bhakti me aate he.
> Example - Parikshit Maharaj, Ambrish Maharaj, Yudhistar Maharaj
> Become anasakt from post
> Use the position for service of Krishna
> Not let the post go inside the mind.
* Jap hum in upadhiyon se mukt ho sakte hai.
> Jap humare mana rupi darpan se agyan ki dhul hat jayegi.
> Tab hum apne apko Krishna ke shashwat sevak ke apne
mulswarup me dekh sakenge.
* Hare Krishna mantra se humara chitt saaf hoga
> Hamari dharane ki hum swami he, hatega.
> Hum bhagwan sewak he, yehe bhavna rahegi.
> Anya Viashnavo ki bhi sewa ki bhavna aayegi.
