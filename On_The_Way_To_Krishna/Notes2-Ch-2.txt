* Bg. 9.30
अपि चेत्सुदुराचारो भजते मामनन्यभाक् ।
साधुरेव स मन्तव्य: सम्यग्व्यवसितो हि स: ॥ ३० ॥

Even if one commits the most abominable action,
if he is engaged in devotional service he
is to be considered saintly because he is
properly situated in his determination.
> भजते मामनन्यभाक् - engaged in devotional service
unto Me without deviation. (for getting
devotional service)
> अपि चेत् - Kadachit purv-abhyas (galati se) se
durachar upasthit ho jaye to
> सम्यग्व्यवसितो - properly situated in determination.
Determination should be like - for doing bhaktiyog if
one has to go hell still a person is doing bhaktiyog.
If Brahma Ji himself asks a person to follow karmyog,
gyanyog etc. still the person does bhaktiyog.
