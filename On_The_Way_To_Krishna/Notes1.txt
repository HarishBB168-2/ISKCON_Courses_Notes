-

> Pathar sukh/dukh ka anubhav nahi karte
* Viksit chetna, aviksit chetna ke apeksha sukh/dukh ka anubhav
adhik gehrai se kar sakti he.

>> Pate hare he
>> Fal ful raha he

- Is sansar ka sukh, saccha sukh nahi he
* Because it is temporary

- Anubhav atma kar rahi he
* Q -> Atma to guno ke pare he. Atma kaise bhautik 
anubhav prapt kar sakta he?
> Vaastvik baat he ki, yehe asambhav he.
> Ye asambhav he ki, atma bhautik bhogo se prabhavit ho.
> Is asambhav karya ko, bhagwan ki maya shakti - avidya,
sambhav kar deti he.
> Is karya ko sambhav karti he - Mithya Ahankar rupi shakti ke dwara.
> Mithya ahankar se jeev apni pehchan bhautik bhogo se aur
mana ki anukul/pratikul vrittiyon se kar raha he.
* Example -> Going to cinema
> Pehli baat to vo kahani jhut he.
> Cinema me aap gaye hi ho, kuch chano ke liye apne aap ko agyan me
dalne ke liye, apne jagrit jivan ko bhulne ke liye.
> Vo movie aap nahi chala rahi, kor vyakti piche baitha 
control kar rha he
> Is movie se aapka koi sambandh nahi he.
> In Cinmea light on curtain is falling - no one is winning/losing.
> Before starting movie there is darkness created in cinema hall
>> Apne swarup ka agyan
> Jo ham nahi he usme abhinivesh - hum hero ko khud samajhne lagte he
>> Jab hero villan ko mar rha he to hum nahi mar rahe hote.
>> Lekin hum khud ko hero maan rahe hote he.
> Hero ke sukh/dukh se hamara koi sambandh nahi he.
> Anubhav isliye ho pa raha he kyonki hum khod ko vo vyakit maan 
rahe he
>> Isi tarah hum khud ko mana man rahe he.
>> Anubhav karne ki chamta mana me nahi he.
>> Mana bhi jad he.

- Indriyon ka suddh hona. But indriya to jad he, vo kaise shuddh ho?
* Indriya to jad he. Suddh kya hoga?
* Chetna ke anusar ham sukh anubhav karte he.
* Indriya suddh ka abhipraya he, chetne aur mana ka suddh hona.

* Adhyatmik-sfuling (atma) me sada anandopbhog karne ka samrthya
rahata he, kintu bhautik avaran ke karan vaha samarthya hamesha 
abhivyakt nahi hota he.

- Atma anand-upbhog kar raha he
* Anand-upbhog karne ke liye indriyon ki jarurat he.
* Isiliye atma ki bhi indriya honi chahiye.
* Bhautik indriya, adhyatmik indriyon ke upar avaran he.
* Lesson :- 
> Bhautik indriyon se samuchit matra me santusht karne wala sukh
nahi milega.
> Isiliye bhautik indriyon me invest karne ke bajaye, vyakti apni
adhyatmik indriyon ko jagrit karne ka prayas kare.
* Jitna hum bhog karenge, utna he adhyatmik indriya aur cover
ho jayengi.
* Bhautik indriyon ka bhog nikrishta, yadyapi vo readily/easily
available hota he. Udhar vyakti ko nahi mana laga karke, 
adhyatmik indriyon ko jagrit karne ka prayas kare.
* Atma anu ke samaan sukhsm he, tathapi vahi vastav me aanand 
ka bhokta he.
* Atma ko naapna-tolna sambhav nahi he, kintu iska arth yaha 
nahi hai ke atma ka koi pariman hi nahi he.
> Just like buddhi can't be measure interms of kg or meters.
* Jiske paas indriya (adhyatmik indriya) he, uska aakar hona hi chahiye. 
Isiliye atma ek vyaktitva he, nirakar nahi.
* Atma ka swarup he, isiliye parmpita parmatma ka bhi swarup he.

- Siddhant

सुखमात्यन्तिकं यत्तद्‍बुद्धिग्राह्यमतीन्द्रियम् ।
वेत्ति यत्र न चैवायं स्थितश्चलति तत्त्वत: ॥ २१ ॥

- Bhautik sukh
* Sukh of sut (son), mit(friend) ramani(patni)
is like a drop of water in desert.
* Is ek bund pani ke sukh ke liye hum barambar
janm mrityu ke chakkar me ghume ja rahe he.

- Saccha sukh
* Sadharan manav nahi janta ki saccha sukh kya he.
* Isiliye vaha sacche sukh se vichlit ho jata he
* Saccha sukh - Bhagwan ki sewa me.
* Jiva avidya se grasit he, to bhagwan ke sukh ko nahi
samajh raha he, vo sochta he isi bhautik sukh se kaam
chala lete he, aur bhagwan ke sukh ke prayas se vichlit
ho jata he.

- Japa kaise karna he
