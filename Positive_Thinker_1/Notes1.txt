Topic : Qualities of True Hero
Speaker : Vivek Goyal Prabhu Ji

- Who is you hero?
* BG 3.21
यद्यदाचरति श्रेष्ठस्तत्तदेवेतरो जनः ।
स यत्प्रमाणं कुरुते लोकस्तदनुवर्तते ॥ २१ ॥

Whatever action a great man performs,
common men follow. And whatever standards
he sets by exemplary acts, all the world pursues.

- Am I a leader?
* Everyone is a leader
> Whether one wants or not
* One need to decide, what kind of leader one wants to be

- Good leader's qualities
* CCD -> Competance, Character, Devotion
* Competance -> Experience, quality, knowledge, goals,
performance, skills
> Competant person attracts & inspires others
> Competance is what we do
* Character -> Is what we are
> Character of Arjun infront of Urvashi in heaven.
* Devotion -> Important to maintain character.
> Devotion of Arjun.
