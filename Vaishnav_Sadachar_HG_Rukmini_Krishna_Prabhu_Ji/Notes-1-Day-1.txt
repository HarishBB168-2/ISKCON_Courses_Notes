- Vaishnav Sadachar
* Jivan shaili of vaishnav
> appearance, eating habit, talking sense etc.
* Punctuality
* This course should be done by every devotee and every 2 years.

- Sadachar
* One meaning
Sada - sarvatha, sarvatra
Achar - Behave like vaishnav
* During outside (non-devotees), some behaviour may change, 
but inside (heart) is same.
* 2nd meaning
Sat-achar - Aisa achar jisase vo satya (Shri Krishna) ko prapt kar sakta hai.
* Akele ya sab ke bhich mai, sab jagah sadachar ka palan karna.
* Srila Prabhupada says that my disciples should be known as perfect gentleman.
* Sadachar should be done using - sharir, vaani and heart.
* Learning
> How to do dandwat pranam.
> How to behave with devotees (senior, junior, equal).
> How to gather for celebrating festival.
> How to make prasadam, distributing and honoring.
> How to go for yatras.
> How to talk with opposite gender.
> How to come to mandir. And what clothes.
> How to behave in mandir. 
> Where and how to do pranam.

- Vaishnav Sadachar book
* Compiled by Devotees of Chowpatty Mandir - Sri Radha Gopinath Mandir

- Krishna ko aakarshit karte hai - vaishnav sadachar ka abhushan dalne se.
* Maryada ulanghan karne vala - iss lok and parlok dono mai nast ho jata hai.

- Shri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu ne Vrindavan ke 6 Goswami ko 5 important updesh pradan kiye
* Inn 5 updesho ke aadhar par hi ISKCON ke badte prachar ke liye niyamo ki rachna ki gai hai.
1. Sabhi shastro ka suksm rup se adhyayan karna and yeh pratipadit karna ki sabhi
shashtro ka mul sar bhakti hai.
2. Bhagwan Shri Krishna ki Vrindavan Lila sthaliyo ko khojna tatha Vrindavan dham ka iss rup
mai nirman karna jisase sampurna vishva ke log vaha aakar sharan le and protsahit ho.
3. Bhagwan Shri Krishna ke sundar mandiro ka nirman karna aur archa-vigraho ki sthapna karke
yogye archna paddhati ke vishai mai sampurna desh ka margdarshan karna.
4. Apne swayam ke aacharan se sikhana ke vaishnav kaisa ho, tatha vaishnav ke shishtachar ke 
example ko prastut karna. Shri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu ke anusar ye atyant important siddhant hai.
Keval shashtro mai nipun hona itna important nahi hai, jitna varisth, kanisth bhakt, bhagwan and
badhjivo ke sath yogya vyavahar ki jankari hona.
5. Apni rachnao and example se ye dikhana ki ek vairagi ka jivan kaisa hona chahiye.
