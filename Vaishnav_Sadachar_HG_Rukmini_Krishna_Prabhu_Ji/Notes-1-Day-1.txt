- Vaishnav Sadachar
* Jivan shaili of vaishnav
> appearance, eating habit, talking sense etc.
* Punctuality
* This course should be done by every devotee and every 2 years.

- Sadachar
* One meaning
Sada - sarvatha, sarvatra
Achar - Behave like vaishnav
* During outside (non-devotees), some behaviour may change, 
but inside (heart) is same.
* 2nd meaning
Sat-achar - Aisa achar jisase vo satya (Shri Krishna) ko prapt kar sakta hai.
* Akele ya sab ke bhich mai, sab jagah sadachar ka palan karna.
* Srila Prabhupada says that my disciples should be known as perfect gentleman.
* Sadachar should be done using - sharir, vaani and heart.
* Learning
> How to do dandwat pranam.
> How to behave with devotees (senior, junior, equal).
> How to gather for celebrating festival.
> How to make prasadam, distributing and honoring.
> How to go for yatras.
> How to talk with opposite gender.
> How to come to mandir. And what clothes.
> How to behave in mandir. 
> Where and how to do pranam.

- Shri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu ne Vrindavan ke 6 Goswami ko 5 important updesh pradan kiye
* Inn 5 updesho ke aadhar par hi ISKCON ke badte prachar ke liye niyamo ki rachna ki gai hai.
1. Sabhi shastro ka suksm rup se adhyayan karna and yeh pratipadit karna ki sabhi
shashtro ka mul sar bhakti hai.
2. Bhagwan Shri Krishna ki Vrindavan Lila sthaliyo ko khojna tatha Vrindavan dham ka iss rup
mai nirman karna jisase sampurna vishva ke log vaha aakar sharan le and protsahit ho.
3. Bhagwan Shri Krishna ke sundar mandiro ka nirman karna aur archa-vigraho ki sthapna karke
yogye archna paddhati ke vishai mai sampurna desh ka margdarshan karna.
4. Apne swayam ke aacharan se sikhana ke vaishnav kaisa ho, tatha vaishnav ke shishtachar ke 
example ko prastut karna. Shri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu ke anusar ye atyant important siddhant hai.
Keval shashtro mai nipun hona itna important nahi hai, jitna varisth, kanisth bhakt, bhagwan and
badhjivo ke sath yogya vyavahar ki jankari hona.
5. Apni rachnao and example se ye dikhana ki ek vairagi ka jivan kaisa hona chahiye.

# Mandir mai palan kiye jane wale niyam
-------------------------------------------------
- Vinamrta
* Mandir mai koi bhi palki mai, vahan mai pravesh na kare.
* Mandir mai jute-chappal pahankar pravesh na kare.
* Bhakto ke sang mai, mandir mai - dasaanudas ki hi bhavna honi chahiye

- Pranam Paddhati
* Haribhaktivilas - written by Shri Sanatan Goswami Ji and Shri Gopal Bhatt Goswami Ji.
All rules are mentioned in this granth.
* Dandwat / Ashtang pranam.
Sharir ke 8 ang -> 2 per, 2 ghutne, chati, 2 hath and mastak
Hath purna rup se mastak ke aage hone chahiye. Unhe chaati ya mastak ke niche na rakhe.
Man ke dwara jisko pranam kar rahe hai, unke baare mai sochna chahiye.
* Panchang pranam.
Sharir ke 5 ang -> 2 ghutne, 2 hath and mastak
* Pranam karte samay pehle Guru Pranam Mantra, Srila Prabhupada pranati and Bhagwan
ke liye pranam mantro ka uccharan kare.

- Mandir mai pranam
* Darshan band hai
Knock kare/ghanti bajae, mandir mai pravesh kare,
Vaishnav baithe hai to "Vancha kalpatru..." bolke PANCHANG pranam kare,
Srila Prabhupada Ji ko DANDWAT pranam kare,
Tulsi Maharani Ji dikhe to unhe bhi pranam kare,
* Darshan khule hai
Knock kare/ghanti bajae, mandir mai pravesh kare, 
Jai Jagannath/Jai Gaur Nitai/Jai Lakshmi Nrisimha Dev Bhagwan ki jai bolke pranam kare (agar
class chal rahi hai to tej bolke disturb na kare),
Vaishnav baithe hai to "Vancha kalpatru..." bolke PANCHANG pranam kare,
Srila Prabhupada Ji ko DANDWAT pranam kare,
Altar ke pas aake left darbar to right darbar ki oor DANDWAT pranam kare (In ISKCON Rohini - 
Shri Shri Panchtatva Ji, Shri Shri Radha Madhav Ji, Shri Ram Darbar Ji) ye mantra bole
> Shri Guru Maharaj Ji, Srila Prabhupada Ji, Archa Vigraha ke (can skip if don't know), 
Panchtatva mantra, Hare Krishna mahamantra.
Tulsi Maharani Ji dikhe to unhe bhi pranam kare,
[Darshan band ho to pranam aur parikrama nahi karna chahiye]
[Half curtain mai bhog lagta hai, to darshan band manana chahiye, deep dan bhi nahi krna chahiye]
* Vaishnav ko pranam
Darshan khule hai - Bhagwan ke samne Vaishnav ko pranam nahi karna chahiye, hath jod
kar Hare Krishna bol de.
Darshan band hai - Vaishnav ko dekh kar unhe turant pranam karna chahiye, kyunki agar 
thodi der mai vo kahi chale gaye, to unko pranam nahi kar payenge.
* Aarti ke time
Don't do parikrama,
Bhagwan ke samne khade hona chahiye, nachna chahiye.
Agar koi vaishnav mandir mai aa jaye and unhe koi receive krne ke liye nahi hai to pujari
ko aarti ko rokakar vaishnav ko recieve krna chahiye.
* Jap karte waqt, mala ungli mai rehte hue pranam nahi karna chahiye. Hath nikalkar
pranam karna chahiye.
* Altar se thoda antar rakhkar hi dandwat kare.

- Vaishnav aadar
* Vaishnav ko vishes paristhitiyo mai pranam na kare
> Snan kar rahe ho, fool tod rahe ho, paani la rahe ho,
prasad grahan kar rahe ho.
> Aise kathi paristhiti mai hum mind mai bhi pranam kar sakte hai. Hare Krishna bhi bol
sakte hai.
> Yogye paristhiti hone par purna dandwat arpit kare.

- Archa-vigraho par dhyan karna
* Pehle pranam karna hai, fir aadar-purvak darshan karna hai.
