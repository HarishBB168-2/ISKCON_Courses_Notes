- Shashtro ka kathan - shashtro ki kathao ke bare mai
* CC Ādi 2.117
siddhānta baliyā citte nā kara alasa
ihā ha-ite kṛṣṇe lāge sudṛḍha mānasa

A sincere student should not neglect the discussion of 
such conclusions, considering them controversial 
(and thinking them beyond understanding), for 
such discussions strengthen the mind. Thus one’s mind
becomes attached to Śrī Kṛṣṇa.
* BG 4.36
Even if you are considered to be the most sinful of 
all sinners, when you are situated in the boat of 
transcendental knowledge you will be able to cross 
over the ocean of miseries.
* BG 4.37
As a blazing fire turns firewood to ashes, O Arjuna, 
so does the fire of knowledge burn to ashes all reactions
to material activities.
* BG 4.40
But ignorant and faithless persons who doubt the revealed 
scriptures do not attain God consciousness; they fall down.
For the doubting soul there is happiness neither in this
world nor in the next.
* BG 4.42
Therefore the doubts which have arisen in your heart out of
ignorance should be slashed by the weapon of knowledge. 
Armed with yoga, O Bhārata, stand and fight.

- Srila Prabhupada ke nirdesh
* मैं हमारे आंदोलन के सभी भक्तो को यह आदेश देता हूँ कि वह मेरी किताबें 
अच्छी तरह से कई दृष्टिकोणों से पढ़े । (Bhargav Prabhu ko likha patra)
> Personal application, preaching application, actual intent
* भक्तों को आपस में लड़ना छोड़कर, सहन करना, जप करना और ग्रंथो का 
अध्ययन करना सीखना चाहिए । (Hridayanand Maharaj ko likha patra)
* हमारे ग्रंथ और मैगजीन माया के विरुद्ध एक बहुत ही शक्तिशाली हथियार है |
(सत्स्वरूप महाराज को लिखा पत्र)
* यदि भक्त दिव्य ग्रंथो का पठन पाठन नहीं करेंगे, तो कुछ समय बाद वह सिर्फ
खाएंगे सोएंगे और पतन के मार्ग पर अग्रसर हो जाएंगे। 
(चैतन्य चरितामृत - मध्य लीला 25.278) 
* मैंने यह ग्रंथ पहले भक्तों के पढ़ने के लिए लिखे हैं, फिर अभक्तों को भक्त 
बनाने के लिए । अतः भक्तों का इन ग्रंथो का पढ़ना अत्यंत आवश्यक है।  
(प्रद्युमन को लिखा पत्र)

- Grantho ke adhyayan par 3 guno ki granthi
* Sato guni adhyayan
> Ekagra, nirantar, bina sangharsh ke hridai mai utarne wala,
anandadayak adhyayan.
* Rajo guni adhyayan
> Mana ka bhutkal ya bhavishyakal mai bhagna, padte vakt hi 
anya karyo ka chintan, apramanik kitabe bhi padna taaki 
dusro ko dikhaya ja sake ki ham bhi koi kam gyani nahi hai,
bina marm ko samjhe jaldi jaldi padna taki kitab puri hone
ka bakhan kiya ja sake.
* Tamo guni adhyayan
> Kitab kholte hi swapn ki dunia mai chale jana, har ek pristh
kala akshar bhais barabar dikhna, kitab ke upar dhul ko bhi
saaf na kar pana, jo kuch bhi pada vaha thode samay baad hi 
bhul jana, trutiya nikalne ke liye adhyayn karna.

- Grantho ko na padne ke kuch bahane
* Dekhiye prabhuji ham to 'bhakt' banane aaye hai na, ham 'gyani'
thode hi banane aaye hai.
* Prabhuji, gyan arjit karna to pracharako ka karya hai na, jo
ki vyas-asan par baith kar pravachan dete hai. Mai thode hi
pravachan deta hun.
* Prabhuji, jyada gyan ho gaya to hum ahankari ho jayenge. 
Ahankari gyani banane se behtar hai vinamra agyani banana.
> Vidya dadati vinayam.
> Gyan pachne par/hridaya mai jane par vinamrta lata hai.
>> Jab Vaishnavo ke sanidhya and unki sewa karenge, samman karenge.
>> Jab naam japa bhi karte hai.
> Gyan head mai pade rehne se aahankar bad sakta hai.
* Guru Maharaj ne sharirik sewa (practical service) par hi dyan
dene ke liye bola hai. 
> Shashtra padna bhagwan ke vaani ki sewa hai.
* Lecture sun liya na MP3 player par / online - bas ap padna kya.
> Sath mai padna bhi jaruri hai.

- Mai granth kyu nahi padh paata/paati hun?
* Jivan bhar kabhi padai ki hi nai. Isiliye aadat hi nahi hai.
> Start ritualistically -> Start with 2 minutes.
* Padne mai aanand nahi aata.
> Har chije maje ke liye nahi hoti.
> Kuch chije out of duty ki jaati hai.
> Purpose/attitude of reading -> To increase our preeti
towards bhagwan.
* Sewa hi itni adhik hoti hai, kya kare?
> Schedule the reading.
* Bas ab jaan to liya hai sab kuch, aur kya padna. ek hi baat 
baar baar likhi hui hai.
> This is called complacency (santriptata ka bhaav).
> Will not be able to receive divya gyan.
> Reading stuti of bhagwan, increases our attraction to bhagwan.
> Reading in bhakti is like breathing for being alive.
* Aas-paas itna shor hota hai, ki padai mai dhyan de hi nahi pate.
> Listen some pleasing music in ear using headphone etc.
> Read early in the morning.
> Just go out somewhere to study.
* Moti-moti kitabo ko dekh kar dar lagta hai.
> Slow and steady and gradually.
* Kuch samajh nahi aata. Sar mai bhayankar dard ho jata hai.
> When we do not have samskar of reading.
> When you start understanding, then this will not happen.
* Mujhe bistar par let kar padna acha lagta hai.
> Bistar padne ka sthan nahi hai.

- Padne ke liye samay kaise nikale.
* Internet se jitna ho sake, vairagya le le.
(Specially facebook, twitter, shorts)
* Idhar-udhar ki gap-shap mai na fase.
* TV naam ke asur ko kabadi vale ko bech de.
* Hamesha apne paas ik kitab avashya rakhe aur din mai jab bhi
samay mile, pade.
* Lambe safar mai public transport ka istemal kare, taki driving
ki chinta chod kar, aap nishchint ho kar pad sake (yadi aapki gadi
mai driver nahi hai to).
* Apne dainik, saptahik aur maasik padai ke ghante ke lakshya rakhe.
(example - ek saptaha mai 10 ghante padna)

- Padne mai ruchi kaise badae (1/3)
* Aise bhakto ka sang kare jo padne mai ruchi rakhte hai.
* Ho sake to ek mala jap kar ke padne baithe.
* Grantho ko vigraha se abhinn manate hue, dhoop agarbatti ityadi se
unki aarti kare aur kripa ki yachna kare.
* Padne ke sthan ko saaf kare. Sato guni vatavaran padai mai sahayak hai.
Bahari vatavaran ka hamare aantarik vatavaran se seedha sambandh hai.
* Table lamp ka prayog se bhi padai kendrit hoti hai.
> Helps in focusing on only book.
* Apne sath highlighter pen rakhe taaki aap mukhya bato ko ek baar fir
se padh sake.
> Do highlighting.
> Make notes on margin.
* Vishai anusaar index bana kar notes banai.
> Example - making topic wise index.
Topics -> Krishna, maya etc.
Find some text about maya then note it down under maya.
Find some text about Krishan then note it down under Krishna.

- Padne mai ruchi kaise badae (2/3)
* Kathin siddhanto ko chitra ya map bana kar samjhe. Example ke liye
Krishna ke avtaro ka varnan.

- Padne mai ruchi kaise badae (3/3)
* Weekend mai saptaha mai jo pada, uski punaravritti (revision)
avashya kare.
> Also in every reading do revision during reading as :-
If you are reading Page 1 to 10.
After reading revise Page 10 to 1.
Then do fast skimming Page 1 to 10.
Then start reading Page 11 to 20.
After reading revise Page 20 to 11.
Then do fast skimming Page 11 to 20.
Then start reading Page 21 to 30.
And repeat the process.
> This increases absorption and comprehension.
* Jo kuch bhi pade use dusro ke sath baate (arthat kirtan/prachar kare).
Bhulne ke is yug (kaliyug) mai smaran shakti ka rahasya hai kirtan 
(ya prachar) karna. Batate batate siddhant aur acchi tarah se samajh mai
aane lagta hai.
